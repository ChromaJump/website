Chroma Jump

Hello!

Chroma Jump is a software/hardware development group. Our current projects are in the area of interactive entertainment e.g. games and tooling.

Our Design Philosophy

  1. Fast
  2. Efficient (low energy footprint)
  3. Fun
  4. Beautiful

At Chroma Jump we:

  • Optimize for all points in our design philosophy where applicable, while prioritizing items higher on the list
  • Write our final products for the platform (and hardware) i.e. native
  • Will use lightweight libraries if necessary and will always try to reduce external dependencies going forward
